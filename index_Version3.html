Our Mission

Charity: Water is a non-profit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Our vision is a world where everyone has access to safe water. Since our founding, we have funded thousands of water projects in dozens of countries.

Projects

Well Construction in Ethiopia

Providing sustainable access to clean water for rural villages in Ethiopia.

Rainwater Harvesting in India

Innovative rainwater harvesting systems for communities facing seasonal droughts.

Clean Water in Haiti

Restoring access to safe water after natural disasters and infrastructure challenges.
